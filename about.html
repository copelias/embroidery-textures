About MAACAT Cuisine

A creative image effects project

MAACAT Cuisine is an experimental creative tool developed as part of the MAACAT project.

The idea behind MAACAT Cuisine is simple: allow anyone to transform images using creative visual effects directly in the browser, without uploading files or relying on external servers.

How it works

All image processing happens locally on your device using JavaScript and HTML canvas. Your images are never uploaded, stored, or shared.

You can:

  • Upload an image from your device
  • Select a creative effect
  • Preview the result instantly
  • Download the transformed image

MAACAT Cuisine is designed for experimentation, inspiration, and creative play. New effects are added over time as the project evolves.

This tool is part of the broader MAACAT ecosystem, focused on creativity, learning, and digital experimentation.
